BUYER DUE-DILIGENCE AND REGULATORY NOTES
Verified on: 2026-07-15

Under the Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Act, 2016, project registration is generally required, with exemptions including projects not exceeding 500 square metres or eight apartments, subject to the Act and applicable state rules.

- Verify the exact project and promoter on the official TG-RERA portal using the stated registration number.
- Compare the registered plans, completion timeline, approvals and quarterly updates with sales material.
- Request the latest sanctioned plan, cost sheet, agreement for sale, payment schedule and possession commitment.
- Do not treat website renderings, travel times, amenity descriptions or third-party prices as guaranteed contractual terms.
- Confirm carpet area, saleable area, parking allocation, maintenance charges, taxes and escalation clauses in writing.
- For legal or financial decisions, use qualified professionals and current official records.

Known information gaps:
- Current unit availability and inventory by tower/floor
- Official price list, base price, premiums, taxes and payment plans
- Confirmed possession dates and construction completion percentages
- Registered promoter legal name for each project, unless retrieved directly from TG-RERA
- Carpet-area values for every unit layout
- Maintenance charges, corpus fund and association rules
- Booking, cancellation, refund, transfer and resale policies
- Home-loan partner list and financing terms
- Any guarantees about travel time, appreciation, rental yield or investment return
